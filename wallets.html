What does Chocolate Coin do??

Chocolate coin is not a complicated chrome extension. I wanted an easier way to see how my cryptos are doing while I am at my desk.

Chocolate Coin allows you to:

○ Track your Coinbase cryptos in dollars, euro or good old pounds.


This extension does not currently allow buying/selling of coins.
